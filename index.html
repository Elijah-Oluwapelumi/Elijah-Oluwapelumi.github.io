Professional Profile

Hi, I'm Oluwapelumi Elijah, a Geospatial Scientist with research interests in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Remote Sensing, Earth Observation, Geospatial Intelligence, and Spatial Data Science. I recently graduated with a First-Class Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) degree in Remote Sensing and Geoscience Information System from the Federal University of Technology Akure (FUTA), Nigeria.

My work focuses on applying geospatial technologies to understand environmental and societal challenges through spatial analysis, satellite remote sensing, and data-driven decision-making. I am particularly interested in integrating GIS, Earth observation data, cloud-based geospatial platforms, and advanced analytical techniques to support sustainable development, environmental monitoring, and geospatial intelligence.

My undergraduate research investigated the geospatial mapping and analysis of smuggling routes along the Saki West border in Oyo State, Nigeria, integrating satellite imagery, digital elevation models, road networks, and multi-criteria spatial analysis to identify areas vulnerable to illegal cross-border activities.
