About Me

I am a First-Class graduate of the Department of Remote Sensing and Geoscience Information System, Federal University of Technology Akure (FUTA), Nigeria.

My interests lie in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Earth Observation, Remote Sensing, Geospatial Intelligence, Environmental Sustainability and Data Analytics.

I enjoy combining satellite imagery, cloud-based geospatial platforms and modern data analytics to solve real-world environmental and security challenges.
